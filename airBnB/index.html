Some more information

Located in Alexandroupoli, 700 metres from Alexandroupoli New Beach and 1.1 km from EOT Beach, Stay home provides air-conditioned accommodation with a balcony and free WiFi. It is situated 1.8 km from Delfini Beach and features a lift. Casino Thraki is 3.2 km from the apartment and Flora and Fauna Museum is 600 metres away.

The apartment with a terrace and quiet street views features 2 bedrooms, a living room, a flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen with an oven and a fridge, and 1 bathroom with a walk-in shower. Guests at this apartment can enjoy wine or champagne and chocolates or cookies. The property has an outdoor dining area.

There is a coffee shop on-site.

Popular points of interest near the apartment include Light House of Alexandroupoli, Saint Kyprianos Church and Sarakatsanoi Museum. Alexandroupoli Airport is 7 km away.
